The Route

The route at a glance

Road 2026 moves from the energy of Madrid to the Riviera light of Cannes and the closing atmosphere of Milan, with each city shaping a different chapter of the experience.

City by City

Each city shapes a different chapter

Madrid
Opening energy, first gathering, and the beginning of the route. The city sets the tone — its rhythm, warmth, and anticipation create the first shared moment.
Bilbao
Sharper movement, architecture, and a stronger road rhythm. The northern landscape introduces contrast — curves, elevation, and a sense of momentum building.
Bordeaux
Elegance, depth, and a slower, more refined atmosphere. Wine country light, stone architecture, and an evening rhythm that invites longer conversations.
Montpellier
Warm southern light, Mediterranean textures, and a lighter pace. The transition point where the journey begins to breathe differently — more open, more relaxed.
Cannes
Riviera glamour, social evenings, and seaside presence. The energy shifts outward — coastal roads, evening gatherings, and the feeling of a journey reaching its peak.
Milan
Design, style, and the final chapter of the journey. The closing city brings reflection, a last shared dinner, and the quiet satisfaction of a route completed together.
